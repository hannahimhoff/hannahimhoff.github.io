Challenge 5: Accesibility Concerns

There were a few accessibility concerns within this site. For example, using white and black pieces with Challenge 2, the chessboard, presented a visual issue. The pieces were impossible to see when on squares of the same color. To mitigate this, I made the black pieces red, and the white pieces blue. This created enough contrast between the pieces and the squares that they clearly can be seen.
